We Care Club

2021-01-30

Hi there! Welcome to We Care Club!

Who are we?

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we saw the society suffering from all the tragedy and turmoil caused by this unexpected deadly virus. We are very distressed by what is going on, so we decided to establish this We care club. Our mission is to connect the people around the world and bring in care, help and love to those in need.

What do we do?

We provide a broad range of activities that mainly focus on counselling and volunteering.
For more information, please navigate to the Activities & Community Service tab.
To see the psychology lesson videos we made, please navigate to the We Care tab.

How to join us?

Join us to gain some valuable volunteer opportunities, leadership and teamwork experience, and most importantly, we need you to help us make greater contributions to our society!!

Lumio Education Intro

2021-01-30 Lumio Education

Online volunteer - Lumio Education

Our club is currently partnering with Lumio Education - a rural-teaching project founded by the Chinese high school students, who are eager to bring more education resources to poverty regions of China.

So far we are providing free education resources for LuKou Village, a poverty region in Jiangsu Province which is located in China.

Lumio Education’s missions (Related Activities):

  1. Promote learning on science and social studies
  2. Academic tutoring
  3. Develop English lessons
  4. Encourage students to read more recommended books
  5. Donate books for students

Teens Teach Technology Intro

2021-01-30 Teens Teach Technology

Online Volunteering - Teens Teach Technology (TTT)

Introduction

TTT is a non-profit Youth organization that aims to teach the seniors and adults who struggle with technology through nursing homes and libraries. Our club has set up a mandarin sub - chapter in TTT, which targets tasks associated with the Asian nursing homes.
